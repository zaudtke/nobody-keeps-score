Suggested Usage

Felt Green

  • bg-felt-600 (nav/header)
  • bg-felt-500 (buttons)
  • text-felt-700 (scores)
  • bg-felt-50 (row hovers)

Card Cream

  • bg-cream-50 (page bg)
  • bg-cream-100 (card bg)
  • border-cream-200
  • text-cream-600 (muted)

Royal Ruby

  • text-ruby-600 (alerts)
  • bg-ruby-100 (badges)
  • ♥ ♦ suit icons
  • danger states

Ink

  • bg-ink-950 (dark bg)
  • bg-ink-900 (dark nav)
  • text-ink-300 (dark body)
  • text-ink-600 (subtitles)

Gold

  • text-gold-500 (winner ♛)
  • bg-gold-100 (highlight)
  • border-gold-400
  • trophy / 1st place
