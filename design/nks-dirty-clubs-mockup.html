Scoreboard — Design Notes

  • Score progress bar: Thin track below each player shows progress to 15. Turns ruby-red when at 13–14 to reinforce the danger zone visually.
  • Bump pips: Small filled dots (one per bump). Empty ghost circles keep the row height consistent regardless of bump count — no layout shift.
  • On board indicator: Small green dot on the avatar corner. Subtle — only matters for Moon eligibility, players at the table will know — but useful reference.
  • Forced play badge: "Must Play" in ruby red appears inline in the name row at 13 or 14. The row also gets a faint ruby left-blush to draw the eye. Hard to miss.
  • Tap arrows (host only): Faint › chevron on the right signals tappability without cluttering the viewer's screen.
  • Not on board state: Score shows at low opacity, "not on board yet" sub-label in italic. Clean distinction from a player with actual points.

Score Entry Sheet — Design Notes

  • Sheet slides up from bottom: Native iOS pattern. Scoreboard remains partially visible above — everyone at the table can still see standings while host enters.
  • Player context at top: Name, current score, and bump count shown in the sheet header — host doesn't have to remember or look back up.
  • 8-button custom pad: DNP (full width, muted) · Bump / Double Bump (ruby tones) · 1–5 (felt green numerals) · Shoot the Moon (full width, special gradient).
  • Moon disabled state: Greyed out with "— not on board yet" hint inline. No separate toast or modal — the information is right there on the button.
  • Moon selected state: The confirm button transforms — purple-to-green gradient, text becomes "🌙 Confirm Moon — Game ends after this hand". High-stakes action deserves a distinct confirmation.
  • Confirm button: Disabled until a selection is made. Prevents accidental empty submits.

Payout Screen — Design Notes

  • Slides up over the scoreboard: Full-screen takeover so the result gets proper attention. "Next Game" sends it back down.
  • Moon win variant: Different hero treatment — deep purple-to-green gradient, moon emoji, "Shoot the Moon!" banner. Feels like a special moment.
  • Breakdown per player: Base $0.25 + bump penalties shown separately so there's no dispute at the table. Bump amount in ruby to draw the eye.
  • Total callout: Gold Fraunces numeral — "Sandra collects $1.15". The winner reads this and everyone pays them directly.
  • Actions: "Next Game" (primary, felt green) routes to Game Setup. "End Night" (secondary) archives the session.
